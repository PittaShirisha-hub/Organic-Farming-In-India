Kerala Organic Farming

Kerala is a major producer of organic spices and coconut in India.

Kerala’s organic farming is renowned for its sustainable practices and high-quality production of spices such as black pepper, cardamom, and nutmeg. The state's organic farms also grow a variety of fruits and vegetables, providing consumers with fresh, pesticide-free produce.

Kerala's farmers focus on eco-friendly practices, conserving soil fertility, reducing water usage, and avoiding harmful chemical pesticides. This approach not only ensures healthier food but also protects the environment and improves the livelihoods of the farmers.
