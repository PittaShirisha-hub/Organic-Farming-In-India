Organic Farming in Jharkhand

Jharkhand promotes organic farming through government schemes and tribal participation, focusing on crops like millets, pulses, vegetables, and medicinal plants using eco-friendly practices.

Organic crops in Jharkhand include millets (ragi, kodo), pulses (arhar, moong), rice, oilseeds (mustard, sesame), vegetables (brinjal, tomato, spinach), and medicinal plants like tulsi and ashwagandha.

Highlights

  • Declared “Organic State” with strong political and administrative commitment.
  • Government Support through schemes like PKVY and HAFED for training, certification, and market access.
  • Focus on Traditional Crops: Millets, pulses, and indigenous rice varieties.

Support Programs

Jharkhand organic farming through the Organic Farming Mission, PKVY, PGS certification, local organic markets, and farmer training programs.
