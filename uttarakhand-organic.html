Organic Farming in Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is a pioneer in organic farming, especially in hilly regions, with strong government, farmer, and NGO support promoting sustainable cultivation of traditional crops, fruits, and medicinal herbs.

Organic crops in Uttarakhand include millets (mandua, jhangora), pulses (urad, rajma), oilseeds (mustard), spices (turmeric, ginger), fruits (apple, litchi, peach), and medicinal plants like tulsi and ashwagandha.

Highlights

  • Declared “Organic State” with strong political and administrative commitment.
  • Government Support through schemes like PKVY and HAFED for training, certification, and market access.
  • Key organic crops include mandua (finger millet), jhangora (barnyard millet), pulses, turmeric, ginger, amaranth, fruits (apple, litchi), and herbs like tulsi and ashwagandha.

Support Programs

Jharkhand organic farming through the Organic Farming Mission, PKVY, PGS certification, local organic markets, and farmer training programs.
