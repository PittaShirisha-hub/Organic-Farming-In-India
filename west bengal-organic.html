Organic Farming in west bengal

West Bengal actively promotes organic farming through government schemes, focusing on sustainable agriculture and the cultivation of traditional crops.

Key organic crops include aromatic rice varieties (e.g., Radhatilak, Kalabhat), tea, turmeric, ginger, and various fruits and vegetables.

Highlights

  • Declared “Organic State” with strong political and administrative commitment.
  • Government Support through schemes like PKVY and HAFED for training, certification, and market access.
  • west bengal natural and traditional farming with minimal chemical use, focusing on eco-friendly cultivation of diverse crops in tribal and hilly regions.

Support Programs

west bengal farming through the Organic Farming Mission, PKVY, PGS certification, local organic markets, and farmer training programs.
