Organic Farming in Telangana

Overview

Telangana is actively promoting organic farming, especially in crops like turmeric, pulses, and cotton. The state's favorable agro-climatic zones and government support have helped organic farming gain momentum in recent years.

Key Organic Crops

  • Organic Turmeric
  • Organic Cotton
  • Pulses

Government Initiatives

Under programs like Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), Telangana is providing training, certification, and market linkages for organic farmers. Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) are playing a crucial role in this effort.
